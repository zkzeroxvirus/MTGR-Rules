# **🛤️ STAY OUT SYSTEM**

The Stay Out System allows players to continue a run without returning to Town, gaining immediate rewards at the cost of increased risk and reduced control.

Staying Out represents pushing deeper into the dungeon with limited resources and increasing uncertainty.

---

## **🎯 WHEN STAY OUT OCCURS**

After an encounter is resolved, players must choose one:

• Return to Town  
 • Stay Out

If players choose to Stay Out, resolve the Stay Out sequence before proceeding to Events.

---

## **⚙️ STAY OUT FLOW**

When the party chooses to Stay Out, resolve the following in order:

1. Gain XP  
2. Gain Mystery Packs  
3. Resolve a Supply Drop  
4. Resolve a Wanderer  
5. Resolve Events  
6. Proceed to the next encounter 

   ---

## **⚡ XP REWARD**

Each player gains XP when choosing to Stay Out.

### **📈 BASE XP**

* Each player gains \+10 XP

### **📈 SCALING XP**

Each consecutive Stay Out increases XP gained by \+5.

Example:

* First Stay Out → \+10 XP  
* Second → \+15 XP  
* Third → \+20 XP  
* Fourth → \+25 XP

Scaling resets when the party returns to Town.

---

## **📚 MYSTERY PACKS**

Each player gains **2 Mystery Packs** when Staying Out.

### **⚡ SCALING**

Mystery Packs do not scale with consecutive Stay Outs.

### **⚖️ RULES**

* Mystery Packs are opened immediately  
* Each player keeps **1 card** per pack — this limit applies regardless of Brands or other effects  
* The only exception is a specific Traveler interaction available in Town  

*(See Merchant in the Town System for full Mystery Pack rules)*

---

## **📦 SUPPLY DROP**

Supply Drops represent scavenged resources or unexpected finds between encounters.

### **🎲 RESOLUTION**

• Roll 1d10  
 • Resolve the corresponding effect from the Supply Drop table  
 (Defined in the Supply Drop System document)

### **⚖️ RULES**

• Supply Drops occur outside of encounters  
 • Effects resolve immediately  
 • Effects do not interact with active battlefield states

---

## **🧳 WANDERER**

Wanderers represent traveling merchants, survivors, or mysterious figures encountered between battles.

### **🎲 RESOLUTION**

• Reveal 1 random Wanderer  
 • Each player may interact with that Wanderer once

### **💸 COST**

• Wanderers require XP to use  
 • Wanderers do **not** consume building uses

### **⚖️ RULES**

• Wanderers occur outside of encounters  
 • Effects are lower impact than Town buildings

Wanderers focus on:  
 • Deck smoothing  
 • Minor upgrades  
 • Resource exchange

• Wanderers do not affect the battlefield directly

---

## **🎲 EVENT MODIFIER**

Staying Out increases Event frequency and scales with consecutive Stay Outs.

After resolving all Stay Out effects:

* **Base:** Resolve **2 Events** instead of 1  
* **Scaling:** Each consecutive Stay Out adds **\+1 additional Event**

Example:

* First Stay Out → 2 Events  
* Second → 3 Events  
* Third → 4 Events  
* Fourth → 5 Events

Scaling resets when the party returns to Town.

Events are resolved **one at a time, in order drawn**. Each Event must fully resolve before the next begins.

---

## **❌ RESTRICTIONS**

While Staying Out:

• Players fully heal and reset to max HP  
 • "Lose/gain X life" affects current life only; permanent health changes must explicitly say **"Max HP/Life"**  
 • Players do not access Town buildings  
 • Players do not gain free building effects

---

##    **🧠 DESIGN NOTES**

Stay Out is intended to be a frequent and viable choice.

It provides:  
 • Immediate XP (scaling +5 per consecutive Stay Out)  
 • 2 Mystery Packs per player  
 • A Supply Drop  
 • A Wanderer  
 • Increased variance through scaling Events

The increased Event count introduces:  
 • Higher unpredictability  
 • Greater potential reward or disruption

---

## **🔧 SYSTEM INTENT**

Town Path:  
 • Lower variance  
 • Controlled progression

Stay Out Path:  
 • Higher variance  
 • Higher potential upside

The Stay Out System reinforces this distinction by increasing Event frequency and granting additional rewards at the cost of control.
The safety tradeoff is no longer HP recovery, but reduced control compared to Town upgrades.
