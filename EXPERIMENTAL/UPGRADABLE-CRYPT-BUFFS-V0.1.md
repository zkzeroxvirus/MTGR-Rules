# **Experimental Upgradable Crypt Buffs V0.1**

Upgradable Crypt Buffs are a proposed Season 2 progression system that allows players to strengthen a Crypt Buff through repeated victories.

Each eligible Buff has a maximum of ten ranks. Reaching Rank 10 unlocks a stronger version of its associated Crypt, called an **EX Crypt**. Defeating the EX Crypt evolves the Rank 10 Buff into its final **Ultimate Buff**.

## **Purpose**

This system gives players additional long-term goals after unlocking a Crypt Buff while preserving a defined endpoint for progression.

The proposed Rank 10 cap is intended to support repeat play without introducing infinitely scaling permanent power.

## **Progression Path**

An upgradable Crypt Buff follows this progression:

1. Defeat its associated Crypt to unlock the Buff at **Rank 1**
2. Earn additional copies or upgrades to increase its rank
3. Reach **Rank 10** to unlock the associated **EX Crypt**
4. Defeat the EX Crypt to evolve the Buff into its **Ultimate form**

The Ultimate Buff is the final stage of progression and does not gain additional ranks.

## **Buff Ranks**

* Each eligible Crypt Buff begins at Rank 1
* Each eligible upgrade increases that Buff by one rank
* A Crypt Buff cannot exceed Rank 10
* A ranked Crypt Buff occupies one Buff slot regardless of its rank
* Rank is tracked separately for each player and each Crypt Buff
* Rank benefits apply only while that Buff is equipped unless its rules state otherwise

Each upgradable Crypt Buff must have its own rank table. Rank effects are not required to use the same numerical progression because Crypt Buffs may affect different game systems.

## **Suggested Power Curve**

The following structure is a design guideline rather than a universal rule:

| Ranks | Intended Progression |
| ----- | ----- |
| 1–3 | Establish and improve the Buff's core effect |
| 4–7 | Provide smaller efficiency, consistency, or frequency improvements |
| 8–9 | Add minor secondary benefits or final numerical improvements |
| 10 | Complete the standard Buff and unlock its EX Crypt |

Effects that grant additional turns, phases, steps, cards, actions, or uses should not automatically increase once per rank. Each rank table must account for the strength and repeatability of its specific effect.

## **EX Crypt Unlock**

When a player reaches Rank 10 with an eligible Crypt Buff, that player permanently unlocks the EX version of the Crypt associated with that Buff.

* An EX Crypt is a stronger version of its normal Crypt encounter
* Reaching Rank 10 unlocks access to the EX Crypt but does not automatically grant the Ultimate Buff
* EX Crypts are intentionally selected and are not added to the normal random Crypt pool unless a future rule says otherwise
* Unlocking an EX Crypt does not consume or reset the Rank 10 Buff

The encounter rules, deck, scaling, and special rewards for each EX Crypt are defined by that Crypt.

## **Ultimate Buffs**

When an eligible player defeats an EX Crypt, their associated Rank 10 Buff evolves into its Ultimate form.

* The Ultimate Buff replaces the Rank 10 version; the two versions do not stack
* An Ultimate Buff occupies one Buff slot
* An Ultimate Buff has no additional ranks
* An Ultimate Buff should provide a defining improvement rather than an unrestricted multiplication of the standard effect
* An Ultimate Buff cannot be downgraded unless a future rule explicitly allows it

## **Duplicate Rewards**

Season 1 Crypt Buffs are currently binary unlocks, and duplicate Buffs are automatically sold for Essence. Upgradable Crypt Buffs require a different duplicate procedure.

The proposed procedure is:

* If the Buff is below Rank 10, an eligible duplicate increases it by one rank
* If the Buff is Rank 10 but its Ultimate form is not unlocked, additional duplicates do not grant the Ultimate Buff
* If the Buff is already Ultimate, additional duplicates are converted into Essence or another defined capped reward

The exact duplicate conversion value remains undecided.

## **Player Eligibility**

Crypt Buff ranks and Ultimate forms belong to individual player profiles.

An EX Crypt may be played by a party containing players with different progression levels. The reward eligibility rule is not yet final. Options for testing include:

* Only players who entered with the associated Rank 10 Buff receive the Ultimate upgrade
* All participating players unlock the Ultimate form if they already own the standard Buff
* Only the player who unlocked or selected the EX Crypt receives the Ultimate upgrade

## **Season Compatibility**

This system is proposed for Season 2 Buffs. Season 1 Buffs remain binary unlocks unless they are individually redesigned with ranks, an EX Crypt, and an Ultimate form.

Upgradable Event Crypt Buffs may use this system if their Seasonal Event rules explicitly enable it.

## **Current Design Questions**

The following rules require discussion or playtesting:

* How Buff upgrades are earned
* Whether every duplicate always grants one rank
* Whether players can target a specific Buff for upgrades
* The power progression for each Buff's ten ranks
* How EX Crypt difficulty scales with party size and progression
* Which participating players qualify for the Ultimate reward
* How duplicate rewards work at Rank 10 and Ultimate
* Whether EX Crypt victories grant additional XP, Essence, or unique rewards
* Whether an Ultimate Buff may be sold and, if so, what happens to its progression

## **Status**

Upgradable Crypt Buffs, EX Crypts, and Ultimate Buffs are experimental Season 2 proposals. No Buff should use this progression system until its Rank 1–10 effects, associated EX Crypt, Ultimate effect, and reward eligibility rules have been defined.
