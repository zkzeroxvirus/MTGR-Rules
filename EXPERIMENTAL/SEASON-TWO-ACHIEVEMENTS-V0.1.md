# **Experimental Season Two Achievements V0.1**

This document contains proposed Season Two Guild, Quirky Tribal, and Mythic Achievements. All effects and acquisition requirements are experimental and require playtesting.

## **General Achievement Rules**

* Achievement progress belongs to an individual player profile
* Unless a requirement says otherwise, all listed requirements for an Achievement must be completed by the same player during a single run
* A tracked action counts only when performed or controlled by that player
* A Crypt victory requirement is completed only if that player remains eligible for the Crypt's victory rewards
* “In your deck” refers to the deck used for the required Crypt victory
* Commander color identity, not the colors of the physical card, determines Guild eligibility
* Each Achievement occupies one active Buff slot when equipped
* “Once per encounter” uses are restored at the beginning of each encounter
* The Host verifies tracked totals until automated tracking exists

The numerical requirements below are recommended starting points for testing. They may be raised or lowered after completion-rate data is available.

# **Guild Achievements (10)**

## **Azorius — Reflective Authority**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, you may return target nonland permanent an opponent controls to its owner's hand.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Counter eight or more spells and/or abilities in a single run
* Exile six or more nonland permanents opponents control in that run
* Win the Crypt fight with a white-blue Commander

**Balance Note**  
The proposed counts are reduced from twelve counters and ten exiled permanents because the two requirements demand different interaction packages in the same deck.

---

## **Boros — Blazing Charge**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, at the beginning of combat on your turn, target creature you control gains double strike until end of turn.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Deal 100 or more total damage to opponents and permanents you do not control in a single run
* Attack with a creature with power 12 or greater in two separate encounters during that run
* Win the Crypt fight with a red-white Commander

**Balance Note**  
The original 150-damage and three-encounter thresholds may make completion depend more on run length and party size than Boros performance.

---

## **Dimir — Secrets Unveiled**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, as a sorcery, target opponent reveals their hand. You may exile a nonland card with mana value 5 or less from it. Until end of turn, you may cast that card, and mana of any type may be spent to cast it.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Mill and/or surveil a total of 20 or more cards in a single run
* Cause opponents to discard and/or mill a total of 15 or more cards in that run
* Win the Crypt fight with a blue-black Commander

**Balance Note**  
Mana value 5 or less prevents the Achievement from taking an unrestricted game-ending card while retaining Dimir's theft identity. Cards milled from opponents may count toward both relevant totals.

---

## **Golgari — Life in Death**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, when a nontoken creature you control dies, you may return target creature card from your graveyard with mana value less than or equal to that creature's mana value to the battlefield tapped.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Have 20 or more creatures you control die in a single run
* Return six or more creature cards from your graveyard to your hand or battlefield in that run
* Win the Crypt fight with a black-green Commander

**Balance Note**  
Twenty deaths and six returns should still require a dedicated graveyard deck without demanding that it repeatedly loop the same pieces.

---

## **Gruul — Untamed Fury**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, at the beginning of combat on your turn, you may have creatures you control gain trample and haste until end of turn.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Declare one or more attackers during every encounter of a single run
* Control a creature with power 12 or greater in two separate encounters during that run
* Win the Crypt fight with a red-green Commander

**Balance Note**  
“Attack in every encounter” is defined as declaring at least one attacker. Power 12 is recommended because requiring power 15 twice favors a narrower set of Gruul builds.

---

## **Izzet — Spellslinger's Gambit**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, when you cast an instant or sorcery spell, you may copy that spell. You may choose new targets for the copy.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Cast 15 or more instant and/or sorcery spells in a single run
* Resolve three or more instant and/or sorcery spells with mana value 5 or greater in that run
* Win the Crypt fight with a blue-red Commander

**Balance Note**  
The large-spell requirement now reinforces the Achievement's copy effect without requiring five six-mana spells from a typical spellslinger deck.

---

## **Orzhov — Debt Eternal**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, as a sorcery, exile target creature card with mana value 4 or less from an opponent's graveyard. Create a token that's a copy of that card, except it is a 4/4 white and black Spirit creature with flying in addition to its other types and abilities.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Gain a total of 30 or more life in a single run
* Create 20 or more tokens in that run
* Win the Crypt fight with a white-black Commander

**Balance Note**  
This wording clarifies which characteristics the token retains. The lower counters still require both Orzhov themes without overly favoring unusually long runs.

---

## **Rakdos — Theater of Chaos**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, choose target creature an opponent controls and flip a coin. If you win the flip, destroy that creature, then you may repeat this process by choosing another creature an opponent controls. If you lose a flip, the ability ends. A creature cannot be chosen more than once during the same activation.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Destroy 12 or more creatures you do not control in a single run
* Deal 100 or more total damage to opponents and permanents you do not control in that run
* Win the Crypt fight with a black-red Commander

**Balance Note**  
The effect remains uncapped because each successful destruction requires another winning flip. The reduced requirements avoid combining two separate high-volume grinds.

---

## **Selesnya — Bloom's Vow**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, if one or more creature tokens would be created under your control, you may create twice that many creature tokens instead. Tokens created by this replacement effect enter with an additional +1/+1 counter on them.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Create 30 or more creature tokens in a single run
* Control 12 or more creatures at the same time in two separate encounters during that run
* Win the Crypt fight with a white-green Commander

**Balance Note**  
This is potentially one of the strongest Guild effects and should be tested with mass-token generators. If it remains an outlier, cap the number of additional tokens it can create at five.

---

## **Simic — Adaptive Evolution**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, as a sorcery, choose target creature you control. Move any number of +1/+1 counters from other creatures you control onto it. Then double the number of +1/+1 counters on the chosen creature and draw a card.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Put 20 or more +1/+1 counters on permanents you control in a single run
* Win an encounter while controlling a creature with power 15 or greater during that run
* Win the Crypt fight with a blue-green Commander

**Balance Note**  
The counters must come from other creatures. If doubling repeatedly produces excessive one-turn kills, replace “double” with “put X additional +1/+1 counters on it, where X is the number moved this way.”

# **Quirky Tribal Achievements (5)**

## **Ouphe Saboteur**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, you may counter target activated ability.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Control two or more Ouphes at the same time
* Cause three or more artifacts you do not control to be destroyed during the same run
* Win the Crypt fight with at least two Ouphe cards in your deck

---

## **Goat Herald**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, until end of turn, whenever a creature you control deals combat damage to a player, untap that creature and put a +1/+1 counter on it.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Control four or more Goats at the same time
* Have four or more Goats you control die during the same run
* Win the Crypt fight with at least four Goat cards in your deck

**Balance Note**  
Untapping does not grant another combat phase. The death threshold is reduced from six because Goat is a limited tribe.

---

## **Octopus Savant**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, when a creature you control with power 8 or greater deals combat damage to a player, you may draw two cards.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Control two or more Octopuses at the same time
* Deal combat damage to an opponent with a creature with power 8 or greater in three separate encounters during the same run
* Win the Crypt fight with at least two Octopus cards in your deck

---

## **Jellyfish Paradox**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, when a creature you control becomes the target of a spell or ability an opponent controls, you may have that creature phase out.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Control two or more Jellyfish at the same time
* Have a Jellyfish you control survive combat damage dealt by a creature with greater power during the same run
* Win the Crypt fight with at least two Jellyfish cards in your deck

---

## **Brushwagg Awakened**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, at the beginning of combat on your turn, target creature you control gets +X/+X until end of turn, where X is its power as this ability resolves.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Control two or more Brushwaggs at the same time
* Attack with a creature whose power became 10 or greater due to one or more pump effects during the same run
* Win the Crypt fight with at least two Brushwagg cards in your deck

## **Quirky Tribal Balance Note**

Requiring a defined number of tribe cards prevents a single incidental creature from satisfying the Crypt condition. Creature types with very small card pools should be checked against the available card source before these minimums become final.

# **Mythic Achievements (5)**

## **Victory Lap**

**Effect**  
Your maximum number of active Buffs increases by one.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Defeat two Crypt bosses during a single session
* Be eligible for the victory rewards from both Crypt fights

**Balance Note**  
The submitted increase of two slots raises the current four-slot limit by 50% and is substantially stronger than other Achievements. Begin testing at one additional slot. A two-slot version should require a more difficult milestone, such as defeating three distinct Crypt bosses or multiple EX Crypts in one session.

---

## **Gambler's Never Quit**

**Effect**  
Once per town, before paying XP for a Town Action, you may flip a coin. If you win, that action costs 0 XP. If you lose, that action costs twice its normal XP cost. You cannot activate this ability unless you can pay the doubled cost.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Resolve ten or more coin flips during a single run
* Win at least five of those coin flips

**Balance Note**  
This measures sustained use of chance mechanics without requiring a statistically rare winning streak.

---

## **Compelling Madness**

**Effect**  
Once per encounter, target player gains 5 life. You draw a card.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Cause a non-Host player to lose the game due to a spell or ability you controlled
* Win a Crypt fight during the same session

**Balance Note**  
Because this requirement involves eliminating another player, it should count only during a game mode or table situation in which player-versus-player effects and the attempt are permitted by the Host.

---

## **Dog's Best Friend**

**Effect**  
At the beginning of deckbuilding, you may choose one of three random Companions. If you do, your completed deck must satisfy that Companion's restriction, and you receive two additional free non-Commander mulligans during deckbuilding. If the completed deck does not satisfy the restriction, remove the Companion and the additional mulligans.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Satisfy a Companion restriction for an entire run
* Cast that Companion during the Crypt fight
* Win that Crypt fight

**Balance Note**  
The additional mulligans apply to deckbuilding card choices, not opening-hand mulligans.

---

## **One with Death**

**Effect**  
During deck creation, you may choose two free cards instead of one. One of those cards may ignore the Game Changer restriction. You cannot choose the same card twice.

**Unlock Requirements**

* Win a Crypt fight on turn two or earlier
* Do not lose life during that Crypt fight

**Balance Note**  
This is an exceptionally difficult requirement paired with an exceptionally strong deckbuilding reward. Retain it as a Mythic test case, but audit qualifying victories for loops or encounter-specific exploits before granting the Achievement.

# **Primary Playtest Concerns**

Record the following information during Season Two testing:

* How often each Achievement is completed per attempted run
* Whether progress scales unfairly with party size or number of encounters
* Whether generated tokens and repeated loops inflate tracked totals
* Average value produced by each once-per-encounter effect
* Whether Bloom's Vow, Adaptive Evolution, or Spellslinger's Gambit routinely ends encounters immediately
* Whether Victory Lap becomes the automatic best Buff-slot choice
* Whether rare creature types can meet their deck-count requirements without relying on Changelings
* Whether players alter play in disruptive ways to satisfy an unlock requirement

# **Status**

These Season Two Achievements are experimental. They should not be added to permanent player profiles until their wording, tracking rules, acquisition thresholds, and interaction with the shared Buff-slot limit are approved.
